JARVIS Core Identity

What should be the central visual element of the HUD?

Arc Reactor (3D + Energy Shader)

Concentric rings with GLSL energy shader. Pulsing core, rotating rings at different speeds. The classic Iron Man arc reactor. Uses Three.js + R3F.

Holographic Sphere (Particle Cloud)

10k particles forming a sphere with hologram shader. Fresnel edge glow, scanlines, wireframe overlay. More organic and AI-like. Uses Three.js + GLSL.

Voice Orb (Organic Blob)

Morphing blob with mix-blend-mode:screen layers. Iridescent, organic, reactive. Less mechanical, more AI personality. Uses Framer Motion + CSS.
